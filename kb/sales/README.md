## Certification check

A sales technician is ready for supervised customer calls when they can:

- Explain ReadyTech without jargon
- Identify the ideal customer and service zone
- Run the 15-minute qualification call
- Match needs to ReadyConnect, ReadyUptime, or ReadyOps
- State approved starting prices and exclusions
- Recognize engineering-escalation triggers
- Explain the Uptime Assessment and Good/Better/Best method
- Review an Instant Estimate and schedule the next step
- Complete a clean sales-to-delivery handoff
