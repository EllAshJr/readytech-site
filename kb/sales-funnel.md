# ReadyTech Sales Funnel

## Standard Funnel

Targeted prospect
→ 15-minute uptime call
→ Paid Uptime Assessment
→ Good / Better / Best proposal
→ Agreement and deposit
→ Installation
→ Managed service
→ Quarterly review
→ Referral

## Website Responsibilities

### Book a Business Uptime Assessment

This is the primary call to action.

### Get an Instant Estimate

This is the secondary call to action.

The Instant Estimate should:

- Qualify the customer
- Educate the customer
- Identify likely service needs
- Provide a reasonable starting estimate
- Encourage scheduling a verification call

The Instant Estimate does not replace:

- Onsite verification
- Final scope
- Final proposal
- Signed agreement

## Proposal Strategy

Always provide three options:

- Good: solve the immediate problem
- Better: solve and monitor
- Best: solve, monitor, and provide continuity

## Payment Strategy

- Collect 50% of project labor before scheduling
- Collect 100% of hardware cost before ordering
- Collect the remaining balance according to the signed scope
- Place managed-service customers on autopay
