# ReadyTech Support Boundaries

## Public Promise

24/7 automated monitoring with human response during published support hours.

## Operating Rules

- Do not advertise a staffed 24/7 NOC.
- Define standard support hours in each agreement.
- Define after-hours rates and approval requirements.
- Promise response targets, not guaranteed resolution times.
- Document included support hours.
- Document monitored devices and services.
- Document excluded systems and devices.
- Third-party carriers and platforms remain subject to their vendors.
- Unsupported equipment may require replacement before monitoring.
- Emergency changes require customer authorization when practical.

## Response Language

Use:

"ReadyTech will begin reviewing the incident within the applicable response
target."

Do not use:

"ReadyTech guarantees the issue will be resolved within the response target."

## Third-Party Systems

ReadyTech may coordinate with:

- Internet providers
- POS vendors
- Cloud platforms
- Payment providers
- Cabling contractors
- Hardware manufacturers

ReadyTech does not control third-party response times or service availability.
