# ReadyTech Sales Quick Reference

**Audience:** Sales technicians
**Read time:** 3 minutes
**Use this when:** Preparing for a call or checking approved language and starting prices.

## One-sentence positioning

> ReadyTech installs, monitors, and supports the connectivity local businesses depend on to operate.

## Primary customer

1–5 location restaurant, retailer, or local service business; no internal IT; revenue depends on internet, Wi-Fi, POS, payments, or cloud apps.

## Service pillars

| ReadyConnect | ReadyUptime | ReadyOps |
|---|---|---|
| Networks, Wi-Fi, VPN, segmentation | Monitoring, failover, backup | POS, restaurant systems, QR, guest Wi-Fi |

## Flagship prices

| Offer | Starting price |
|---|---:|
| Uptime Assessment | $349 |
| ReadyConnect Foundation | $1,500–$4,500 + hardware |
| ReadyUptime Managed | $249/location/month |
| ReadyContinuity Pro | $449/location/month |
| Restaurant Add-on | $250/location/month |

## Standard rates

- Remote: $125/hour
- Onsite: $150/hour, two-hour minimum
- After-hours: $200/hour
- Deposit: 50% labor
- Hardware: 100% prepaid

## Qualification essentials

```text
Problem → Business impact → Locations → Critical systems
→ Current support/vendors → Authority → Timeline → Budget → Onsite need
```

## Primary close

> “The next step is the $349 Uptime Assessment. It verifies the environment and gives you Good, Better, and Best options.”

## Escalate when

Multi-site complexity, more than 20 devices, more than five APs, unknown/legacy equipment, major cabling, overnight cutover, compliance claims, custom integration, or guarantees.

## Support promise

> 24/7 automated monitoring with human response during published support hours.

Never promise a staffed 24/7 NOC or guaranteed resolution time.

## Geographic language

- Austin–Manor: core onsite zone
- Houston: extended remote/scheduled project zone

## Funnel

```text
Qualify → Assess → Propose → Deposit → Install → Manage → Review → Refer
```
