# Sales Coach Context

## Purpose

Internal sales application at:

/sales

The Sales Coach is designed to shorten the sales learning curve.

It should:

- guide the salesperson
- prompt questions
- decide what card/path to use next
- recommend the right offer
- explain why
- tell the salesperson the next step
- email the completed report to the salesperson and owner with an HTML report attachment

## Desired Salesperson Experience

Salesperson listens.
App thinks.
Salesperson persuades.
App documents.
Salesperson follows next step.
Owner reviews result.

## Recommendation Outcomes

Possible recommendations:

- ReadyTech Uptime Assessment
- ReadyConnect Foundation
- ReadyUptime Managed
- ReadyContinuity Pro
- Restaurant Operations Add-on
- Engineering review
- Low-fit / referral / follow-up

## Primary Next Step

Book a Business Uptime Assessment unless the customer is low-fit, too complex, or needs engineering review first.

## Sales Coach Should Not Yet

- collect payments
- auto-send proposals to customers
- promise final pricing
- guarantee resolution times
- replace engineering review
