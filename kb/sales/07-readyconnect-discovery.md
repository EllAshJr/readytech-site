# ReadyConnect Discovery: Network, VPN & Wi-Fi

**Audience:** Sales technicians
**Read time:** 7 minutes
**Use this when:** Qualifying secure networks, business Wi-Fi, VPN, remote access, or segmentation.

> **Sales outcome:** Determine whether the customer needs a standard ReadyConnect Foundation, a site survey, or engineering review.

## Business questions first

- Which people and systems must connect?
- What fails today—coverage, speed, access, security, or stability?
- Is the business opening, expanding, moving, or replacing equipment?
- Does downtime affect payments, staff, customers, or remote work?

## VPN and remote-access discovery

Ask:

- Remote access, site-to-site, or both?
- Number of remote users and locations?
- Existing firewall/router brand and model?
- MFA required?
- New VPN, working VPN, or currently broken?
- Monitoring and after-hours cutover required?

Tier guidance:

- 1–5 users: Remote Access Essentials
- 6–15 users: Business Remote Access
- Two sites: Site-to-Site VPN
- Three or more sites: Multi-Site / engineering review

## Business Wi-Fi discovery

Ask:

- Square footage, floors, indoor/outdoor areas?
- Current access-point count and brands?
- Estimated concurrent devices at peak?
- Staff Wi-Fi, guest Wi-Fi, or both?
- Dead zones, drops, slow areas, or roaming issues?
- Existing Ethernet cabling?
- Guest portal, QR access, filtering, or speed limits?

Do not guarantee access-point count from square footage alone. Walls, materials, interference, ceiling height, and density matter.

## Segmentation discovery

Identify separate groups such as:

- POS/payment
- Staff
- Guest Wi-Fi
- Cameras/IoT
- Kitchen or operations
- Back office

Explain simply:

> “Segmentation keeps guest and noncritical devices separated from systems that process payments or run the business.”

## Site-survey triggers

- Unknown cabling
- More than five APs
- Outdoor coverage
- Dense customer areas
- Multiple floors or unusual construction
- Repeated dead zones
- New build or major remodel

## Recommended next step

Unknown environment: Uptime Assessment. Clean standard installation: ReadyConnect Foundation. Complex routing, RF, or multi-site design: assessment plus engineering.
