# Service Pillars & Flagship Offers

**Audience:** Sales technicians
**Read time:** 6 minutes
**Use this when:** Matching a customer’s business problem to the right ReadyTech offer.

> **Sales outcome:** Sell outcomes through three pillars and four flagship offers—not a catalog of disconnected technical tasks.

## Three service pillars

| Pillar | Customer outcome | Typical services |
|---|---|---|
| **ReadyConnect** | Connect the business properly | Networks, Wi-Fi, VPN, remote access, segmentation |
| **ReadyUptime** | Keep the business operating | Monitoring, backup internet, failover, managed backup |
| **ReadyOps** | Improve daily operations | POS readiness, restaurant technology, QR tools, guest Wi-Fi |

## Four flagship offers

### 1. ReadyTech Uptime Assessment — **$349**

Entry point for most new prospects. Includes internet/firewall review, Wi-Fi health, business-device or POS review, backup-internet readiness, risk summary, top three improvements, and a fixed proposal. Credit the assessment toward projects above $2,000 when approved within 30 days.

### 2. ReadyConnect Foundation — **$1,500–$4,500 + hardware**

Installation project for a clean foundation: router/firewall configuration, business and guest Wi-Fi, segmentation, secure remote access when needed, documentation, testing, and handoff.

### 3. ReadyUptime Managed — **from $249/location/month**

Essential recurring plan: internet and firewall/router monitoring, up to 10 devices, outage alerts, monthly summary, vendor coordination, and business-hours response.

### 4. ReadyContinuity Pro — **from $449/location/month**

Recommended plan for uptime-sensitive sites: up to 20 devices, firewall/switch/AP/VPN monitoring, backup internet management, monthly failover testing, priority response, one included remote support hour, and quarterly review.

## Restaurant Operations Add-on — **from $250/location/month**

Add to ReadyContinuity Pro for POS network monitoring, supported printers/KDS, delivery-app coordination, guest Wi-Fi, QR/loyalty support, and vendor coordination. Typical starting total: **about $699/location/month**.

## Match the pain to the offer

| Customer says | Lead with |
|---|---|
| “We do not know what is wrong.” | Uptime Assessment |
| “The network is messy or being rebuilt.” | ReadyConnect Foundation |
| “We need alerts and basic monitoring.” | ReadyUptime Managed |
| “Downtime directly costs us money.” | ReadyContinuity Pro |
| “POS, kitchen, delivery, or guest systems fail.” | Restaurant Add-on |

## Cross-sell rule

A project should create a path to monitoring. A monitoring customer should be reviewed for backup internet. A restaurant monitoring customer should be reviewed for the Restaurant Operations Add-on.
