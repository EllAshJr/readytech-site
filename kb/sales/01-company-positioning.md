# ReadyTech Positioning & Sales Mission

**Audience:** Sales technicians
**Read time:** 5 minutes
**Use this when:** Explaining who ReadyTech is, opening a sales call, or writing outreach.

> **Sales outcome:** The prospect should understand that ReadyTech protects business operations—not that we are a generic computer-repair company.

## The message to remember

**ReadyTech installs, monitors, and supports the connectivity local businesses depend on to operate.**

Our category is **business uptime and connected operations**. We focus on the systems tied to revenue and daily work: internet, Wi-Fi, POS connectivity, remote access, monitoring, backup internet, managed backup, and restaurant technology.

## What ReadyTech is—and is not

| ReadyTech is | ReadyTech is not |
|---|---|
| A business-uptime partner | A generic “fix my computer” shop |
| Installation plus ongoing management | Residential IT as a primary market |
| Plain-language technical coordination | A staffed 24/7 help desk or NOC |
| Restaurant-led, business-wide | A restaurant-only company |
| Focused on connectivity and continuity | A custom software-development firm |

## Use customer-outcome language

Lead with business impact:

- “Keep POS, Wi-Fi, payments, online orders, and cloud systems operating.”
- “Reduce outages and vendor confusion.”
- “Install it correctly, monitor it continuously, and respond when business depends on it.”

Avoid opening with acronyms, firewall brands, VLANs, or VPN protocols. Technical detail belongs after the business problem is understood.

## Core tagline

> **Installed right. Monitored continuously. Ready when business depends on it.**

## 20-second introduction

> “ReadyTech helps local businesses keep the connectivity they rely on working. We install secure networks and business Wi-Fi, monitor critical devices, manage backup internet and remote access, and coordinate restaurant and POS connectivity. We start with an Uptime Assessment so the solution matches the real environment.”

## Primary next step

The default call to action is:

> **Book a ReadyTech Uptime Assessment.**

The Instant Estimate is a qualification and education tool. It does not replace final verification, scope, or proposal.

## Sales technician checkpoint

Before moving forward, you should be able to explain ReadyTech in under 30 seconds without using the words *VLAN*, *protocol*, *topology*, or *managed services provider*.
