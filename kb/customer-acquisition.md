# ReadyTech Customer Acquisition

## Direct Outreach Targets

Build prospect lists containing:

- Independent restaurants
- Fast-casual restaurant groups
- Retail stores
- Salons
- Local service businesses
- New commercial tenants
- Businesses opening a second location

## Business-Outcome Message

Use:

"ReadyTech helps businesses keep POS, Wi-Fi, payments, online orders, and cloud
systems working when internet or network equipment fails."

Do not lead with:

- VLAN terminology
- VPN protocols
- Firewall models
- Technical acronyms

## Referral Partners

Build relationships with:

- POS sales representatives
- Low-voltage cabling contractors
- Electricians
- Internet-service representatives
- Commercial real-estate brokers
- Restaurant consultants
- Security-camera installers
- Bookkeepers serving local businesses
- Web and digital-marketing agencies

## Partner Value

ReadyTech solves the connectivity and uptime problems that prevent the
partner's customer from using the partner's product or service successfully.

## Founding-Client Offer

For the first ten managed clients:

- Paid Uptime Assessment
- Assessment credited toward an approved project above $2,000
- Managed-service price locked for 12 months
- Priority onboarding

Do not heavily discount recurring managed-service plans.
