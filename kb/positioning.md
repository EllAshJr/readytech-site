# ReadyTech Positioning and Pricing Policy

## Company Category

Business uptime and connected operations.

## Main Promise

ReadyTech installs, monitors, and supports the connectivity local businesses
depend on to operate.

## Primary Customer

Independent restaurants, fast-casual operators, retailers, and service
businesses with one to five locations and no internal IT department.

## Restaurant Position

Restaurants are ReadyTech's first specialization, but not its only market.

## Service Pillars

### ReadyConnect

Connect the business properly:

- Secure business networks
- Business and guest Wi-Fi
- Remote access
- VPN
- Network segmentation

### ReadyUptime

Keep the business operating:

- Network monitoring
- Backup internet
- Internet failover
- Managed backup
- Secure file access

### ReadyOps

Improve connected operations:

- POS readiness
- Restaurant technology
- Guest Wi-Fi
- QR tools
- Vendor coordination

## Flagship Offers

- ReadyTech Uptime Assessment: $349
- ReadyConnect Foundation: $1,500–$4,500 plus hardware
- ReadyUptime Managed: from $249 per location/month
- ReadyContinuity Pro: from $449 per location/month
- Restaurant Operations Add-on: from $250 per location/month

## Pricing Policies

- Remote support: $125/hour
- Onsite support: $150/hour with a two-hour minimum
- After-hours support: $200/hour
- Project labor deposit: 50%
- Hardware: 100% prepaid
- Managed-service term: 12 months
- Published prices are starting points
- Final scope follows the Uptime Assessment
- Hardware, carriers, cabling, permits, subscriptions, and third-party fees are
  separate unless included in writing
