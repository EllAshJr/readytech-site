# Business Strategy

## Category

Business uptime and connected operations.

## Three Pillars

### ReadyConnect

Connect the business properly.

Services:

- Networks
- Business Wi-Fi
- VPN
- Secure remote access
- Segmentation

### ReadyUptime

Keep the business operating.

Services:

- Monitoring
- Backup internet
- Failover
- Managed backup
- Secure file access

### ReadyOps

Improve daily operations.

Services:

- POS readiness
- Restaurant technology
- QR tools
- Guest Wi-Fi
- Customer-experience technology
