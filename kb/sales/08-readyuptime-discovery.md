# ReadyUptime Discovery: Monitoring, Failover & Backup

**Audience:** Sales technicians
**Read time:** 7 minutes
**Use this when:** Qualifying monitoring, backup internet, business continuity, or managed backup.

> **Sales outcome:** Identify what must remain available, what should be monitored, and how quickly ReadyTech should respond.

## Monitoring discovery

Ask:

- Which systems stop business when unavailable?
- Number of routers/firewalls, switches, APs, servers, POS devices, printers/KDS, and other critical devices?
- Business-hours or 24/7 automated alerting?
- Email or SMS alerts?
- Monthly reporting required?
- Standard or priority response?

Tier guidance:

- Up to 10 devices: ReadyUptime Managed
- Up to 20 devices + failover/priority: ReadyContinuity Pro
- Larger or multi-site: engineering review/custom proposal

## Backup-internet discovery

Ask:

- Current ISP and outage history?
- LTE, 5G, second wired ISP, or unsure?
- Which systems must remain online?
- Required backup speed?
- Existing cellular router or carrier plan?
- Automatic failover and monthly testing required?
- Maximum acceptable downtime?

Positioning:

> “Backup internet is not only a second connection. It must be integrated, tested, monitored, and understood by the business.”

Keep carrier fees separate.

## Managed backup and secure file access

Ask:

- Number of users, workstations, and servers?
- Current storage and expected growth?
- Existing data migration?
- Backup frequency and retention?
- Remote access and restore assistance required?

Do not position ReadyTech as the underlying cloud operator. Sell management of established backup and secure-file platforms.

## Support-boundary language

Promise:

> “24/7 automated monitoring with human response during published support hours.”

Do not promise a staffed 24/7 NOC or guaranteed resolution time.

## Strong ReadyContinuity indicators

- Payments or POS require internet
- Repeated ISP outages
- Multiple critical devices
- No one tests failover
- Owner demands faster response
- Business has no internal IT

When business interruption has a clear cost, lead with ReadyContinuity Pro—not the minimum plan.
