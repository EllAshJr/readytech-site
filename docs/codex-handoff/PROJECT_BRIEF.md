# Project Brief

## Brand

ReadyTech

## Domain

readytechinstalls.com

## Market

Austin, Manor, and Houston.

## Category

Business uptime and connected operations.

## Core Positioning

ReadyTech installs and manages the connectivity local businesses depend on:

- Secure business networks
- Business Wi-Fi
- Remote access
- Monitoring
- Internet failover
- Managed backup
- Restaurant technology

## Short Homepage Message

Technology that keeps your business connected, protected, and ready.

## Tagline

Installed right. Monitored continuously. Ready when business depends on it.

## Strategic Focus

Restaurants are the first specialization, not the whole company.

ReadyTech should serve:

- Independent restaurants
- Fast-casual operators
- Retail stores
- Service businesses
- Small offices
- Multi-location local businesses

Avoid making the brand look like residential IT, generic computer repair, or restaurant-only technology.
