# ReadyTech Service Delivery

Every customer should follow the same service-delivery process.

## 1. Qualification Call

Target duration: 15 minutes.

Confirm:

- Business type
- Number of locations
- Main business problem
- Urgency
- Decision-maker
- Rough budget range
- Whether onsite work is required

## 2. Paid Uptime Assessment

Deliver:

- Current-state summary
- Risk findings
- Recommended solution
- Good / Better / Best proposal

## 3. Three-Option Proposal

### Good

Fix the immediate business problem.

### Better

Fix the issue and monitor the environment.

### Best

Fix, monitor, and provide business continuity.

## 4. Agreement and Payment

Collect:

- Signed scope of work
- 50% labor deposit
- 100% hardware payment
- Managed-service agreement when applicable
- Autopay authorization when applicable

## 5. Implementation

Follow this checklist:

1. Inventory the environment
2. Back up the current configuration
3. Install and configure
4. Test the solution
5. Document the environment
6. Obtain customer acceptance

## 6. Managed-Service Onboarding

Record:

- Device inventory
- Network diagram
- Vendor contacts
- Internet carrier information
- Customer support contacts
- Monitoring thresholds
- Escalation procedure
- Covered and excluded systems

## 7. Customer Review

- Conduct a 30-day onboarding review
- Resolve remaining installation issues
- Confirm monitoring coverage
- Review backup and failover behavior
- Schedule quarterly reviews for applicable plans
