# Instant Estimate Context

## Purpose

Customer-facing intake and estimate system.

## Role

The Instant Estimate should:

- qualify and educate
- collect customer requirements
- estimate setup and monthly pricing
- email the estimate
- push customers toward scheduling a verification call

It should not replace final assessment or final proposal.

## Desired Flow

Customer enters business details
→ selects services
→ answers service-specific questions
→ receives estimated setup and monthly cost
→ schedules verification call
→ accepts, rejects, or requests follow-up

## Primary CTA

Schedule My Verification Call

## Secondary CTA

View Estimate / Request Revised Estimate

## Important Principle

Published and generated prices are starting points.

Final pricing follows ReadyTech Uptime Assessment.
