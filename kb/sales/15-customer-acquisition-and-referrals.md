# Customer Acquisition & Referral Partners

**Audience:** Sales technicians and business development
**Read time:** 7 minutes
**Use this when:** Building pipeline rather than waiting for inbound leads.

> **Sales outcome:** Create consistent conversations with businesses that have uptime pain and partners who encounter connectivity problems.

## Direct outreach targets

- Independent restaurants and fast-casual groups
- Retail stores
- Salons and local service businesses
- New commercial tenants
- Businesses opening a second location
- Professional offices without internal IT

## Outcome-first message

> “ReadyTech helps businesses keep POS, Wi-Fi, payments, online orders, and cloud systems working when internet or network equipment fails.”

Do not begin with hardware brands or acronyms.

## Short outreach script

> “Hi, I’m ___ with ReadyTech. We install and monitor business-critical connectivity for local operators. I’m reaching out because businesses with POS, guest Wi-Fi, cloud apps, or multiple vendors often have no one responsible for uptime. Who handles network and internet reliability for your location?”

## Referral partner targets

- POS sales representatives
- Low-voltage cabling contractors
- Electricians
- Internet-provider representatives
- Commercial real-estate brokers
- Restaurant consultants
- Security-camera installers
- Bookkeepers serving restaurants
- Web/digital-marketing agencies

## Partner value proposition

> “You provide the primary product or service. ReadyTech makes sure the customer’s network, Wi-Fi, internet, and connected devices are ready and monitored so your implementation succeeds.”

## Founding-client offer

For the first ten managed clients:

- Paid Uptime Assessment
- Assessment credit toward approved project above $2,000
- Managed-service price locked for 12 months
- Priority onboarding

Do not heavily discount monthly service.

## Weekly activity standard

Example starting cadence:

- Add 25 qualified prospects
- Make 20 direct outreach attempts
- Complete 5 meaningful conversations
- Ask 3 partners for introductions
- Book 2 qualification calls
- Review pipeline every Friday

Track meetings and assessments—not only email volume.
